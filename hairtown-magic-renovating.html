The Role of Peptides
Key Ingredient Science

Peptides: the body's
cellular messengers.

Peptides are chemical messengers that give our cells their instructions. They are short chains of amino acids — the same fundamental building blocks that make up proteins — arranged in a specific sequence that tells the cell exactly what to do and when.

In the context of hair, every peptide contains amino acids that are organised into sequences associated with supporting the keratin structure of hair — the structural protein that gives hair its strength, elasticity, and resilience. When hair is damaged by chemical services, heat, or mechanical stress, these keratin bonds break down. Peptides work by delivering amino acid sequences that may help support the hair's natural keratin structure.

Simple to use.
Impossible to ignore.

The Magic Renovating System integrates seamlessly into your existing salon service. The only procedural change is the 4-minute activation window — everything else stays exactly the same.

Phase 1 — Pre-Service · Magic Renovating Spray
01

Prep & Mist

Mist hair with vaporised water to approximately 30% moisture — lightly damp, not saturated.

02

Apply Spray

Divide into sections. Apply Magic Renovating Spray section by section — 2 to 6 sprays per section depending on the hair's nature and characteristics.

03

4 Min · Do Not Rinse

Leave on for 4 minutes to allow the formula to absorb into the hair. Do not rinse. Use this pause to prepare your technical service mixture.

Phase 2 — Technical Service
04

Dry if Required

If your technical service requires application on dry hair, blow-dry before proceeding.

05

Apply Bleach Mixture

Apply the bleaching or colour mixture as normal. Magic Renovating does not inhibit lift and requires no change to oxidant volume.

Phase 3 — Post-Service · Magic Renovating Mask
06

Shampoo Only

Wash hair with shampoo. Do not use conditioner at this stage.

07

Towel Dry

Towel-dry the hair before applying the mask.

08

Apply Mask

Apply Magic Renovating Mask. Amount depends on the client's hair length and characteristics.

09

4 Min · Do Not Rinse

Leave on for 4 minutes before proceeding with styling. Do not rinse.

10

Style

Proceed with styling. If using additional styling products, wait until the full 4-minute processing time has passed.

✦
Toning Service Note

When performing toning services, apply the toner over the Magic Renovating Mask. Allow the toner to process, then wash and rinse. Towel-dry the hair and apply Magic Renovating Mask a second time. Leave on for 4 minutes, then proceed with styling.
